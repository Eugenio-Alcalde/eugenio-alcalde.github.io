Career Profile

I currently work as a Postdoctoral researcher in a European project focusing on the exploitation of the yeast Xanthophyllomyces dendrorhous as a sustainable platform for the production of high-value carotenoids. Last ten years I have been studying the chemical and molecular basis of the sexual interaction in mucorales fungi (Phycomyces Blakesleeanus, Blakeslea trispora and Mucor circinelloides), and also working in the carotenoids and apocarotenoids production and regulation in filamentous fungi and yeasts. I have experience working in six different labs in four countries learning from and adapting to diverse conditions, points of view and expectancies. I have been always willing to learn and apply new techniques to improve my research.
